Coca-Cola Game Jam

Myself and a team of three other programmers took part in the Coca Cola game jam to create a game about crisis management within 48 hours. The game works similar to a cookie clicker/idle game where the player must collect money overtime and spend their cash on factories, skills and upgrades. As the theme was crisis management we designed a system in which random events will occur during the game in which the player will have to make a decision on how they want to handle said event, the player is rewarded or penalised depending on how they deal with the random event. Within the project I was in charge with developing a fully functional skill tree utilizing unityâ€™s scriptable object system.

The game was a winner in both categories, Best Playable Prototype and Best Concept. This included a cash prize.
