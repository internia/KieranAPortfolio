Self Driving AI Car

I created a self driving car AI in Unity utilizing neural networks in conjunction with genetic algorithms in order to train the car. The aim of the application was to allow the car to perform better overtime by training itself in a controlled enviroment.

To train the neural network the application employs the use of genetic algorithms to store and breed higher performing agents throughout generations, genetic algorithms have many real life examples and are based on how human genetics work.

Genetic Algorithms (GA's) are perfect for the application as it can be used to learn the best hyper-parameters for a neural network (NN) as hyper parameters are the only values that a NN cannot learn unless coupled with a learning algorithm such as GAâ€™s.
