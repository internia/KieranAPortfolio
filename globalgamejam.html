Global Game Jam 2019

Myself, two programmers, an audio engineer and an artist took part in the 2019 Global Game Jam to create a game in 48 hours, the theme for the year was "home". To fit the theme we decided to create a game in which the player can decorate their apartment with cozy decorations.

The game had three different "decor paths" in which the player could go down nature, art and computing. Each path has unique decor tailored to the corresponding theme for example if the player picks nature they will get a plant pot on the kitchen counter whereas someone picking the computing decor path will receive a computer on their desk.

Follow the link to download and play the game: https://globalgamejam.org/2019/games/make-your-own
